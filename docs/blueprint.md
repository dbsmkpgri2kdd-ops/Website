# **App Name**: SmartSchool App

## Core Features:

- School Information Display: Display key information about the school, including its vision, mission, address, contact details, and principal's message.
- Dynamic News Feed: Display news articles pulled from a RSS feed or CMS. Also announce school-wide announcements.
- Online PPDB Form: Implement a user-friendly online form for new student registration, capturing necessary details and storing them for admin review.
- AI-Powered Information Assistant: AI Chatbot to answer the parents question regarding registration, facilities, and other school related general questions. The bot uses school FAQ as a tool.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest green (#386641) to symbolize growth and stability.
- Background color: Off-white (#F0F8F4) to provide a clean and calming backdrop.
- Accent color: Terracotta (#A77B5B) to add warmth and highlight key elements.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' serif for titles to create an inviting aesthetic
- Body font: 'Alegreya' serif for main contents to make a pleasant reading experience.
- Use consistent, simple icons from Lucide to represent different sections and actions.
- A clean, grid-based layout to ensure information is well-organized and easy to find. Use clear sections for school info, news, and registration.
